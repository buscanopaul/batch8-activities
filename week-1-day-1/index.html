“Smush” or "Resize" your images
#Being a developer, I always found that optimizing my images for the web wasn’t easy. I tried the good old “Save for web” Photoshop command, but most of the time, I ended up with images that were either too big or without a sufficient quality.
@The higher your resolution, the larger the file size will be on the server. @Big images and slow load times are especially annoying for mobile visitors. @Name the image file correctly to help your SEO (Search Engine Optimization) @PNG for transparent, JPEG for static image, GIF for animated images.
